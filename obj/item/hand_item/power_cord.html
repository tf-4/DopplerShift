proc
do_power_draw
(/obj/target, /mob/living/carbon/human/user)

Runs a loop to charge an android from a cell or APC. Displays chat messages to the user and nearby observers.

Stops when:

  • The user's is full.
  • The cell has less than the minimum charge.
  • The user moves, or anything else that can happen to interrupt a do_after.

Arguments:

  • target - The power cell or APC to drain.
  • user - The human mob draining the power cell.
