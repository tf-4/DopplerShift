/proc/autopunct_bareEnsures sentences end in appropriate punctuation (a period if none exist) and that all whitespace-bounded 'i' characters are capitalized. If the sentence ends in chat-flavored markdown for bolds, italics or underscores and does not have a preceding period, exclamation mark or other flavored sentence terminator, add a period. (e.g: 'Borgs are rogue' becomes 'Borgs are rogue.', '+BORGS ARE ROGUE+ becomes '+BORGS ARE ROGUE+.', '+Borgs are rogue~+' is untouched.)
